Les Plus Grands Succès De Céline Dion

Title : Les Plus Grands Succès De Céline Dion
Released: September, 19 1984
Sales: 90,000 gold in Canada (50,000)

At age 16 Céline already released hr first Greatest hitst album. We find on this album 10 of her greatest hits in the previous 3 years. Part of the profits from this album went to research for Cystic Fobrosis. To really emphasize the good cause of this album, it was launched in a childerens hospital (Sainte Justine, Montréal) and was sond in supermarkets so it was easy to find for people

When you got this album brand new you will soon find a letter written bij Céline to ask you to give the CCFF some money to help them.
