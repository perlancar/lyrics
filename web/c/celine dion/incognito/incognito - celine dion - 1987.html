Incognito

Title: Incognito
Released: April, 2 1987
Sales: 715,000 (2x platinum in Canada (200,000)

Here's the caterpillar who became a butterfly. Her first album with CBS, which became Sony. This is also the first album with different versions from one country to another. A perfect sound, a quality production and the new looks a young woman more decided then ever to take her place in the world of the pop music. All the fears of losing her first public following vanished with only the sound of her voice. Never had an album delivered so many hits. After the release of this album she gave a tour troughout Québec including 42 shows in the St Denis Theatre in Montréal. With her talent and thanks to the succes of Incognito Celine also won the Felix award for best preformance in 1988 for her Incognito tour.
