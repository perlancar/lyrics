D’eux

Title : D’eux
Released : March, 28 1995
Sales :5x platinum in the EU (5,000,000) 8,500,000 7x Platinum in Canada (700,000)

We can write pages and pages about this historic album. But we can also limit ourselves to saying that, on rare occasions, artists meet and create a masterpiece, and this album is really a masterpiece.Jean-Jacques Goldman and Céline re-wrote the record book of French music. He wanted to write for her golden voice, and she wanted to have the recognition she deserved in France. Together the made the album we'll still talking about for dacades to come.Céline said in 1996 that it was really the album of her life, that she could end her career right now, and be proud of what she had done. D'eux was distributed in the USA as The French Album
