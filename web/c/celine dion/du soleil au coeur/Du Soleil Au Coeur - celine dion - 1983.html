Du Soleil Au Coeur

Title:Du Soleil Au Coeur
Released:1983
Sales:75,000 Gold in France (50,000)

This is Céline's first album in France, it's a melting pot from her first albums in Québec (exeptions made of her Christmas album of course). In France, it's a succes as fast as in Québec; with D'amour Où D'amitié she became the first Canadian artist to have a gold record in France. At this time 500,000 copies were needed to certify gold, Celine sold over 700,000 copies. Unfortunately, her succes in France disappeared almost as quickly as it happend.. and came back many years later.
