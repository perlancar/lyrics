Falling Into You

Title : Falling Into You
Released : 12 maart 1996
Sales : 7x platinum in the EU (7,000,000) 11x platinum in de VS (11,000,000) diamond inCanada (1,000,000)

Her fourth English album. There are many coversongs on this album: It's All Coming Back To Me Now, Falling Into You, All By Myself, River Deep, Mauntain Hight and (You Make Me Feel Like) A Natural Woman. In adition to the cover songs there are also 3 English adaptions from D'eux: If That's What It Takes, I Don't Know, and Fly It the first album to deliver 3 top 5 songs , with Because You Loved Me, It's All Coming Back To Me Now, and All By Myself
