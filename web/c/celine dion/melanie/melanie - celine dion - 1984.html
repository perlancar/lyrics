Mélanie

Title: Mélanie
Released: October, 15 1984
Sales: 175,000 Platinum in Canada (100,000)

Praised by the public and the critics, this album won the Félix award for the album of the year and for best selling album. Her gold record in France the year before and the succes of the song Une Colombe, written especially for the Pope's visit, were part of the huge success. The song Mélanie, touched most Québecers, and was inspired by her niece Karin who was fighting cistic fibrosis. The album was pressed a second time and the cardboard center was not the same colour (wend from white to red) and the shape of the CBS logo was also changed.
