Au Coeur Du Stade

Title : Au Coeur Du Stade
Released : 14 september 1999
Sales : 2x platinum in France (600,000) Gold in Canada (50,000)

recorded at the Stade de France, where soccer World Cup of 1998 was held, during Céline concerts of June 19 and 20 , 1999,in front of almost 90,000 people each night. This album is the audio part of one of the greatest concerts ever. It's a suprise Céline sings some of her old time hits like Ce N'etait Qu'un Rêve, D'amour Où D'amitié and Mon Ami M'a Quitée

There is also a videocassette and a DVD available of this album
