Let’s Talk About Love

Title : Let’s Talk About Love
Released : 18 november 1997
Sales :9x platinum in the EU (9,000,000) Diamond in the USA (10,000,000) Diamond inCanada (1,000,000)

After the best selling album Falling Into You wich was on the theme of cover songs, this album is under the theme of contributions, and Céline had the best people you could dream of to join her. Could you imagine a music lover who wouldn't buy an album featuring Céline, Barbra Streisand, Luciano Pavarotti, Carol KIng and the BeeGees? This album has it all! Plus many new songs sung by the Adult- Contemporary #1 singer of the Decade : Céline Dion!
