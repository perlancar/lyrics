Les Chemins De Ma Maison

Title:Les Chemins De Ma Maison
Released: September, 7 1983
Sales: 150,000platinum in Canada (100,000)

Supported by a tour and a TV special that album solidified the career of Céline in Québec. This album was the best seller (all categories) in Québec that year, thanks to the great hits like Mon Ami M'a Quitée and Les Chemins De Ma Maison.
