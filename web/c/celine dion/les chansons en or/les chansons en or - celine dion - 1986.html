Les Chansons En Or

Title: Les Chansons En Or
Released: 1986
Sales: 225,000 platinum in Canada (100,000)

This album futures her biggest hits, including her very first song Ce N'etait Qu'un Rêve. In addition to these 10 hits, we find a brand new song Fais Ce Que Tu Voudras. This song was made into the very frst video of Céline in French, it was taped in Germany. In this video we see Céline with short hair letting uss guess what's coming, but we had to wait another year to see how important the change was.
