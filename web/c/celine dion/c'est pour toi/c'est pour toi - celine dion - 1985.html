C’est Pour Toi

Title: C’est Pour Toi
Released:1985
Sales: 60,000 gold in Canada (50,000)

Her last complete album with new ,aterial before her first year off. During that year , she had changed her musical style, her looks and ofcourse her recording label, joining the CBS Records team. This album features one of the favorites of Céline, Elle and 2 songs from her resent album in France: Les Oiseaux Du Bonheur and Paul Et Virginie (which became Virginie.. roman D'amour For the Québecer market.)
