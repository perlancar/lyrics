Tellement J’ai D’amour Pour Toi

Title:Tellement J’ai D'amour
Released:1982
Sales: 125,000 , Platinum in Canada (100,000 verkochte albums)

This is the album that really established Céline in Québec. All the mothers were touched by the song Tellement J'ai D'amour Pour Toi wich Celine won in Tokyo at the Yahama Song Contest. The song D'amour Où D'amitié was a huge hit, and even today most Québecers can hum it. With this album Celine also won her first four Felix Awards.
