S’il Suffisait D’Aimer  (1998)

Title: S’il Suffisait D’Aimer
Released: 8 september 1998
Sales: 2x platinum in the EU (2,000,000) 4x Platinum inCanada (400,000)

This is another album written almost completely by Jean-Jacques Goldman. There past collabration D'eux The French Album ha became a classic in the French-speaking music world, beeing by far the best-seller of all times! This one is very good too. This album features Céline singing an engaging song in Zora Sourit and a great blues in Tout Les Bleus Sont Écrits Pour Toi in addition to great ballads. In there first collabration, Goldman wrote songs for Céline. This time he wrote more in his own style, while letting Céline sing in her own unique way, as she had been singing a long time ago. Most of the arrangements on this album are simple to let Céline's voice dominates the songs.
