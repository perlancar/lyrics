Les Oiseaux Du Bonheur

Title: Les Oiseaux Du Bonheur
Released: 1984
Sales: 75,000

Second album in France, using once again songs from her previous Quebecs albums, three new songs Paul Et Virginie, Les Oiseaux Du Bonheur and Hymne À L'amitié. Even helped with an excellent preformance as opening act for Patrick Sébastien at the Olympia, the album didn't have a big succes in France and that county had to wait three years more before seeing another album from Céline being released in France.
