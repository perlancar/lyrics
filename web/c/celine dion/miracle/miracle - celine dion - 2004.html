Miracle

 

Title : Miracle
Released : October, 11 2004
Sales :Not yet known by us

Anne Geddes and Céline bring you an incredible collection of images and songs with Miracle, a unique collaboration between two world-class artists. Miracle is a rare display of tenderness and feeling. This compelling, powerful, and inspiring collection of songs and images honors the moving, tender, and unbreakable bond of love that exists between a mother and her baby.

In France the CD comes with a bonus track Je Lui Dirai
