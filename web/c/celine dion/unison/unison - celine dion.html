Unison

Titel : Unison

Released : april 2, 1990
Sales: Gold in France (100,000) 6x platinum in Canada (600,000) en 1x platinum in the USA (1,000,000)

A very important album for Céline's career. It was a dream come true for Céline; her dream was to sing for everyone, and now this album opens the world to her. The song Where Does My Heart Beat Now was a hit all over the world. She proved with this album that she could export her voice, that she feared neither the languages nor the different cultures. Céline said this album was like a second career debut. With about ten albums in French as background and all the energy of her fans supporting her since her very beginning to help her reach the hightest achievments, Céline shot and scored! A videocassette is available (in French and English). In Argentina (AR), it was released as Unisono and the song titles were translated into Spanish on the jacket even if the tracks were still in English
