La Voix Du Bon Dieu

Title:La Voix Du Bon Dieu
Released:November 6, 1981
Sales: about 75,000 although it is not certified officially

The very first steps of a child in the show business world. She had the voice to seduce Eddy Marney, who wrote most of her first songs. Marney even went into writing music for the title song La Voix Du Bon Dieu. The words and music are simple, but we can already hear a distinguished voice. We also find on this album the song that was sent to René Angélil, Ce N'etait Qu'un Rêve. We were hearing without being fully conscious the birth of the biggest star Québec ever had. Please note the 2 editions, the LP was pressed for a second time under a different label.
